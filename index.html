Eclipse, Inc. is a general contracting company.

We are based in Cloquet, Minnesota, and serve clients in Duluth, Superior, and throughout the greater Twin Ports area.

We specialize in:

  • VRF (variable refrigerant flow) heating and cooling systems

    • Instead of traditional HVAC systems, Eclipse focuses on VRF systems for their energy-saving potential. We have worked on these systems since 2008, when we installed a VRF system in the First National Plaza building in Cloquet. This job was part of a research project by Minnesota Power, and was one of the first installations of such a system in the state. You can read Minnesota Power’s Research Profile on this VRF system here. To see how Eclipse can help you with installation or maintenance of a VRF system, please contact us.

  • Painting, both interior and exterior, with color-matching capabilities

    • Using Benjamin Moore’s Gennex Color Technology, we are able to do a precise scan of an existing color sample, mix our own paint with our in-house tinting machine, and produce the right amount of paint needed for your custom job. Contact us if you have a paint job you need done professionally.

  • CNC services, including custom cabinetry, architectural detailing, and more

    • With our ShopSabre industrial CNC router, we can cut woods, plastics, and other materials up to 4’x8’ in size. Computer numerical control allows us to make cuts with extreme precision and reliability. Whether its a one-off custom job, or an order of 100s of the the same part, contact us to see how our CNC team can cut your pieces quickly and accurately.

  • And more…

    • We have poured concrete, hung drywall, installed security fences, erected metal stairs, replaced insulation, ........, and much more. To learn if Eclipse is well suited for your project, please contact us.

  • Eclipse installed a new VRF system in Minnesota Power’s Cloquet office building, replacing an old and ineffective HVAC system. The project was highlighted by Mitsubishi Electric as a case study, you can read more here.
